Tanga Ibuki (丹花イブキ) is one of the students studying in Gehenna Academy who wields an Assault Rifle.

She is a member of the Pandemonium Society.


Profile

Introduction

Ibuki is the energetic mascot girl and the councilor of the Pandemonium Society. She is a well-behaved girl who works hard today so as not to be a nuisance to her seniors! Her fellow club members like to dote on her and will do anything to punish whoever makes her sad.

Personality

Tanga Ibuki is the energetic youngster of the Pandemonium Society who serves as both its mascot and its councilor. Natural with her young age, she is quite child-like, but her age does not determine her strength, as she is an incredibly hard worked who aims to help her seniors in any way she can.

Appearance

Ibuki has blonde hair tied into lopsided twintails. Her eyes are a golden color. She has a visible pair of wings and a heart-spaded tail, with a small pair of black horns on her head being shielded by her hat, making her one of, if not, the only Gehenna student to bear all of these traits at once (horns, wings, tail), unlike other Gehenna students who tend to possess one, two or none of these traits.

Halo

Ibuki's halo is an orange/yellow circle with four evenly spaced spikes and a rhombus at the bottom that extends through the circle. There's an additional arrowhead opposite of the rhombus, sitting at the top of the halo.

Uniform

Ibuki wears a Pinafore school uniform. Over it is a oversized dark-colored military-style jacket with red accents and gold buttons, with the jacket having red hems. She also wears a black peaked cap with a cute pink ribbon on it and yellow short boots.

Firearm

She uses a white early model H&K 416 assault rifle with a 10.4' barrel. The magwell, top and bottom rails, flip up iron sights, and gas block are painted red.
